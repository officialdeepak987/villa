  • Private Pool Experience in Paradise

    Experience luxury at its finest staying in your own private pool villa on Havelock Island. Whether you are sunning yourself on a day of lounging, or taking a midnight dip with your partner under the moonlight it is all yours; no crowds, no curfews, just you!.

  • Surrounded by the Natural Beauty of Havelock Island

    You can wake up to the gentle sounds of the ocean and the song of exotic birds that inhabit the unique natural environment found here. Located deep within the tropical character of Havelock Island, the private villas sit nestled between lush green vegetation, coconut trees, and pristine white sandy beaches.

    The private pool villas have the unique blend of beautiful interior finishes, luxury bed linens, technology, and aesthetic beauty; but the villa experience is just one aspect of the experience. Explore in-villa dining options, experience concierge services in the villa and, experience components of the culture in the space which exemplifies an authentic and unique experience of what the Andaman Islands are.

Discover Nemo Cafe & Sea Dragon Restaurant: Seafacing Dining at Havelock Island

Try the Nemo Cafe and Sea Dragon Restaurant, located right on the beach at Havelock Island Beach Resort. Nemo Cafe accommodates a casual, fun dining scene that is very different from the more formal luxury dining experience at Sea Dragon Restaurant, but each meal will have a distinct, memorable atmosphere that will be part of your essence tropical holiday. Underwater Adventure

Underwater Adventure at Havelock Island: PADI Certified Diving

Live the experience of a lifetime beneath the water from your private pool villa at Havelock Island Beach Resort. The resort's PADI Dive Centre offers some of the best diving and snorkeling excursions on the island for experienced and first time snorkeling and diving experiences on the island. Swim over the spectacularly colourful coral reefs and swim with exotic sea creatures in the underwater paradise of the Andaman Sea. Whether you want to do a legitimate PADI qualification, join a regular day trip for diving and snorkeling, or just hire equipment for the day, our trained professionals will honour you with memories to last a lifetime beneath the sea.

  • PADI Certified Diving Courses for All Levels
  • Daily Guided Dive and Snorkeling Excursions
  • Full Equipment Rental with Latest Gear
